m&mf Healing others¶

This addon helps you use Healing on others via m&mf.

Aliases¶

cre <person>
Cures the person, use this alias if they are in the room with you.
fc <person>
Cures a person remotely, use this alias if they are in an area with you.
fcs
Stops curing a person of their affliction - m&mf will diagnose once and then start curing the affs it remembered on them.
ra <affliction>
Puts up an appropriate aura for an affliction on you.
ra <affliction> <person>
Puts up an appropriate aura for an affliction on an ally.
